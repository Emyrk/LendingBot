How does HODL.ZONE work?

Poloniex offers users the ability to lend their deposits to traders who wish to trade on margin. We use the API of this service and use our bot to automate this process for you. Here's how it works

  1. Create a Poloniex account and secure it with 2FA
  2. Deposit Bitcoin into your Poloniex account
  3. On HODL.ZONE, input your Poloniex API Keys
    • (We can never withdrawal your coins, nor hold custody. They always remain in your control, on Poloniex.)
  4. Enable lending.
  5. Sit back and grow your crypto-holdings.
